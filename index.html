About Me

I am a dedicated Civil Engineering graduate from Sylhet Engineering College (affiliated with Shahjalal University of Science & Technology), with a CGPA of 3.36/4.00. My passion lies in structural engineering, seismic response prediction, and structural health monitoring using advanced tools like Deep Neural Networks and software such as Abaqus and ETABS. I aim to contribute to innovative research and engineering solutions.

Skills

  • Structural Engineering Software: Abaqus, OpenSeesPy, ETABS, AutoCAD
  • Programming & Data Analysis: Python, Machine Learning (Data-Driven Modeling)
  • Structural Analysis: Time History Analysis, Seismic Response Prediction, Structural Health Monitoring

Projects

  • Undergraduate Thesis: Data-Driven Prediction of Seismic Drift Responses Using Deep Neural Networks (DNNs)
  • Structural Design Project: G+07 Story Building (GPH Ispat-Prothom Alo En-genus Competition)

Publications

  • Conference Paper (ICERIE 2025): Use of Time History Analysis of Steel Frame for Enhancing Structural Health Monitoring

Research Statement

My research focuses on leveraging data-driven approaches, particularly Deep Neural Networks, to predict seismic drift responses and enhance structural health monitoring. I am eager to explore innovative methodologies in structural engineering, aiming to contribute to resilient infrastructure design through advanced computational techniques.
